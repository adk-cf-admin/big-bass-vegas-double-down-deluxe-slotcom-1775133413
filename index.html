Three In-Bonus Modifiers: Dynamite, Hook & Bazooka

Adding an extra layer of excitement, Big Bass Vegas Double Down Deluxe includes three random modifiers that activate during the Free Spins round. These modifiers are designed to create productive bonus scenarios, bridging gaps between fisherman wilds and money fish to keep the action flowing and the win potential alive. The modifiers are not triggered on a fixed schedule but activate based on the current reel state, making each bonus round feel dynamic and unpredictable.

  • Dynamite Modifier: Activates when at least one Fisherman Wild is present on the reels but there are no Money Fish to collect. The Dynamite adds Money Fish symbols to the grid, ensuring the fisherman can collect prizes and progress along the multiplier trail.
  • Hook Modifier: Triggers when Money Fish are present on the reels but no Fisherman Wild has appeared to collect them. The Hook pulls a Fisherman Wild onto the grid, allowing the cash prizes to be collected and counted toward the multiplier upgrade trail.
  • Bazooka Modifier: Activates whenever at least one Fisherman Wild is on the reels. The Bazooka randomly transforms regular symbols into different ones (excluding Fisherman Wilds), potentially converting low-value card symbols into higher-paying fish symbols or additional Money Fish prizes.
